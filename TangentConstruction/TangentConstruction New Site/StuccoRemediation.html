This is how a Tangent job looks.


First, we remove the old stucco to assess the damage to framing.

Plastic visqueen is installed to protect the home from the outdoor elements.

We repair framing as needed.

Tyvek is added as a water vapor barrier over wood.

Wire lath is added to the framing areas to give the stucco mud something to adhere to.

The stucco layer is added finally, as well as texture.

Last, the house is caulked and painted.
